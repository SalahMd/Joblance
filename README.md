# joblance

Jobs and  freelancing  portal.
A Flutter app made for companies to post jobs or tasks and freelancers to find jobs or tasks to do.
## Summary

Consists of 2 types of users: Freelancer and Company.
Freelancers can find jobs to work in cpmpanies or tasks to do to other freelancers or companies.
Companies can post jobs or task when users can only post tasks.
## Features

Responsive screens.\
Authintication:full authintication methode like: email verefication, forgot password, resend code verification, etc...\
Products and projects: companies can add products and freelancers can add thier projects.\
Notifications:Fire base notifications.\
Localizations:Arabic and English.\
Dark-mode.\
Google sign-in.\
Favourite.\
Reviews and rates.\
Generate-CV with AI.\
Reports and technical support.\
Paypal payment.\
